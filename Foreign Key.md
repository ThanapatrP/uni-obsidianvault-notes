- Keys that are [[Candidate Key]] in *another [[Relation]]*
- [[Domain]] of these Keys can be *null* and *composite* key

### Keywords
- *"Referencing Relation"* 
	  -> [[Relation]] that are *referencing* [[Candidate Key]] of the "Referenced" [[Relation]]
- *"Referenced Relation"*
	  -> [[Relation]] that their [[Candidate Key]] got *referenced* in another [[Relation]]
